## [0.2.0] - POS & Cash Management Milestone

### Added
- Full POS checkout workflow
- Product search with click or Enter-to-add
- Keyboard-first POS product entry
- Basket quantity controls (+ / - / remove)
- Customer search and attach to sale
- Card and Cash tender support
- Cash tender amount input with change due calculation
- Sale completion success panel
- New sale reset flow

### Cash Management
- Register open / close lifecycle
- Cash movements (paid-in / paid-out)
- Blind register close
- Cash reconciliation foundation

### Receipts
- QR code receipt upload flow
- Mobile receipt upload page
- Receipt attachment to cash movements
- Receipt preview modal
- Receipt open in new tab

### POS Ergonomics
- Automatic focus on product search
- Enter key adds first product result
- Fast-add product flow
- Smooth new-sale reset

### Infrastructure
- Playwright E2E coverage for POS and cash flows
- Frontend/backend integration improvements
